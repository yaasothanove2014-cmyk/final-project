The Story of Hitori

What is Bocchi the Rock! about

Hitori "Bocchi" Gotoh is a lonely high school girl who loves playing the guitar. She spends her days playing alone at home, hoping to one day join a band. Despite her incredible skills as "guitarhero" on the internet, her extreme social anxiety makes it impossible to make friends. Her life changes forever when she is invited to join Kessoku Band by the energetic drummer Nijika Ijichi.

Series Summary

The journey of Kessoku Band is more than just music; it's a comedic and heartfelt exploration of conquering personal hurdles. From the basement stage of STARRY to the chaotic heights of school festivals, Bocchi slowly finds her voice through her guitar strings, proving that even the most anxious person can shine under the right lights.
